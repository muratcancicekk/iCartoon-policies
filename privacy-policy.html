iCartoon Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information collected, processed, and stored when you use the iCartoon application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our App:

  • Account Information: When you register for the App, we may collect information such as your name, email address, and password.
  • User Content: Content you create on the App, including comics, stories, and characters.
  • Usage Data: Data related to your use of the App (which features you use, interaction time, etc.).
  • Device Information: Technical information such as device type, operating system, and device identifier.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To manage your user account
  • To provide technical support
  • To develop the App and design new features
  • To obtain usage statistics

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Service providers we work with to support our App (such as hosting, analytics, etc.).
  • Legal Requirements: When necessary to fulfill a legal obligation, protect our rights, or in a legal process.
  • User Permission: With your permission or at your direction.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, but please be aware that no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights Regarding Your Data

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or transfer the information we have collected about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information below.

7. Changes

We may make changes to this Privacy Policy. When we make significant changes, we will notify you and publish the updated policy in the App or on our website.
